To cite package CITAN in publications please use:

Marek Gagolewski (2011). Bibliometric impact assessment with R and the CITAN package. Journal of Informetrics. In press, doi:10.1016/j.joi.2011.06.006.

A BibTeX entry for LaTeX users is:

@Article{,
   title = {Bibliometric impact assessment
            with {R} and the {CITAN} package},
   author = {Marek Gagolewski},
   journal = {Journal of Informetrics},
   year = {2011},
   url = {http://www.ibspan.waw.pl/~gagolews/CITAN/},
   doi = {10.1016/j.joi.2011.06.006},
}

For more information please visit the CITAN homepage.

In case of any questions or suggestions, feel free to contact the author.
